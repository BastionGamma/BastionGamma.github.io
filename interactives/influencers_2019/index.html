Influencers 2019

As outlined in the Gartner L2 Intelligence: Social Platforms & Influencers 2019 Report, a best practice is to group influencers into cohorts based on follower counts, including advocate (less than 5K followers), micro (5K to 25K followers), small (25K to 100K followers), medium (100K to 250K followers), large (250K to 1M followers), mega (1M to 7M followers) and celebrity (greater than 7M followers).
