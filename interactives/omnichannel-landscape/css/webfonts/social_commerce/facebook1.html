Facebook — "Shop Now" Button

Among features that link to shoppable brand pages, the most straightforward and most frequently adopted option is Facebook’s Shop Now button.

This prominent button connects users from a brand’s Facebook page to whichever site the brand decides, with Shop Now destinations ranging from the brand’s site to third-party retailers.

The feature is easily accessible for both brands and users, making it very popular. Over half of the Index brands analyzed have adopted this button.

A brand’s homepage is by far the most popular Shop Now destination, making up 77% of landing pages. Other brand site options, such as category pages or product pages, make up only 8% of the link destinations.

While this feature favors DTC brands, brands that distribute through e-tailers can still take advantage of Facebook’s linking features to link to informational content or third-party retailers. Among analyzed brands, 4% direct users to a store locator.
