Facebook — Dark Posts

Dark posts are targeted ads that do not appear on a brand's timeline or in all of their followers’ feeds on Facebook and Instagram.

The only tell for users is a small Sponsored tag at the top of the post.

When advertisers buy targeted ads through Facebook’s Power Editor, they can import audiences, use lookalike audiences based on outside data or serve highly targeted demographic segments.

While most dark posts are primarily a reach vehicle, they can also include a variety of features that allow users to link to product pages, and sign up for newsletters and take polls.
