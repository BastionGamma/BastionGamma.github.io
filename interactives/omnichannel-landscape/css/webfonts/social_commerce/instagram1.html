Instagram — Shoppable Pages

Shoppable products on Instagram profiles are easily accessed through the Shop button located directly underneath the brand’s bio.

Clicking on a post with the shopping bag icon reveals a post that allows the user to view the products within it.

Tapping on the products reveals a traditional product page, with individual product pages featuring price and a product description.

Half of observed brands employ Instagram’s shoppable pages.

Not surprisingly, adoption of Instagram’s shoppable features is concentrated among DTC brands, with zero adoption from auto, beverages and consumer packaged goods (CPG) brands.
