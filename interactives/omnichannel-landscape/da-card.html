1. Display advertising offers one of the clearest use cases to push omnichannel agendas. Brands can use programmatic advertising to target consumers based on location, notifying them of nearby stores. They can also retarget customers after visiting sites with product ads that double as vehicles to promote free shipping perks. It’s no surprise, then, that over 14% of digital display ads referred to omnichannel.

  4. Traditional retailers devote a much larger share of display ads to omnichannel, in relation to other sectors. For example, one-quarter of department store ads addressed omnichannel, compared to fashion ads’ 4%. Large-format retail utilizes display, given its power to drive conversions as shoppers browse the web and to boost awareness of fulfillment capabilities in a highly-competitive environment.

  5. Case Study

    Nordstrom doubles down on omnichannel within display advertising, promoting free shipping and in-store pickup in many of its display ad creatives, including seasonal and product ads.Some product ads encouraged conversion by including mass personalization language, like “popular near you.” Nordstrom also highlighted an instore collaboration with MCM, urging viewers to “Pop-in @ Nordstrom.” Overall, two-thirds of its display ads referenced an omnichannel theme during the study period — well above the 25% average among department stores.
