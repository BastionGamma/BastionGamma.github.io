  5. Case Study

    Kate Spade sends store emails to an average of 6% of its audience, resulting in open rates up to 42%. Resulting in this segmentation strategy, the brand witnessed a 20% open rate lift among these emails. In another sign of its refined strategy, the brand sent its largest share of omnichannel emails during the critical Q4 holiday period in 2017.

    Emails address upcoming store events, such as a panel discussion in New York, store closings or new openings. A particular email tries turning a recent store closing into an engagement opportunity, directing those affected to nearby stores.
