Despite the dizzying changes in the market, retailers have responded with a renewed sense of urgency to catch up to the digital-first innovators.

For example, adoption of in-store pickup during check-out climbed 49% year over year, driven by big box, department stores and specialty retail. Other essential features have also increased, such as Buy Online, Pickup in Store (BOPIS) and listing store services.

Furthermore, retailers are progressing in other areas. For example, 67% of watches & jewelry brands showcase inventory instocks on the Product Display Page (PDP) — a significant bump from last study’s 33%.
